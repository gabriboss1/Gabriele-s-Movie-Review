Summary

Inception is a mind-bending sci-fi thriller directed by Christopher Nolan that explores the world of dreams and the subconscious. The film follows Dom Cobb (Leonardo DiCaprio), a skilled thief who specializes in extracting secrets from deep within the dream world. Cobb is offered a chance at redemption when he's tasked with performing "inception"—planting an idea into someone’s mind. With a team of experts, he delves into layers of dreams, facing personal demons, shifting realities, and high-stakes challenges. Blending action, emotion, and thought-provoking concepts, Inception keeps audiences questioning the nature of reality until its iconic final moment.
